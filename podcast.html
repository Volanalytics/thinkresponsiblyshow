Podcast Coming Soon

Stay tuned for the Think Responsibly Show podcast, where we'll explore media literacy, civic engagement, and critical thinking in the digital age.

What to Expect

Host Romonte O. Hamer will guide you through thoughtful discussions with experts, civic leaders, and everyday citizens who are making a difference. Each episode will provide practical insights and tools to help you navigate our complex information landscape.

The podcast will launch in Summer 2025 and will be available on all major podcast platforms including Spotify, Apple Podcasts, Google Podcasts, and more.

Upcoming Topics Include:

  • Recognizing propaganda techniques in modern media
  • How to have productive conversations across political divides
  • Digital privacy and its impact on civic participation
  • Understanding data and statistics in news reporting
  • The role of social media in shaping public discourse
  • Media literacy education for different age groups
