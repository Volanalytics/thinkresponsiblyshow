About Romonte

Romonte O. Hamer created Think Responsibly Show to guide citizens in critically evaluating information and engaging in civic life. With a background in media studies and civic education, Romonte is passionate about fostering a more informed and engaged citizenry.

Through the Think Responsibly Show, Romonte provides tools, resources, and analysis to help people navigate today's complex information environment and participate meaningfully in democratic processes.
